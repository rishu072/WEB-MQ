Flight Ticket Booking with ixigo

Comparing and booking flight tickets online has never been easier. With ixigo, you can compare fares across leading travel booking sites and find the cheapest available flights to any destination. Enter your source, destination, and departure dates, and compare fares from multiple carriers immediately.

Compare Flight Prices Across Airlines

Compare real-time schedules and prices across domestic airlines including IndiGo, Air India, Akasa Air, and SpiceJet. We list the rates directly so you don't miss out on special deals or standard prices. Check flight rankings based on parameters like duration, number of stops, and customer ratings.

Tips to Get Cheap Flight Tickets

Book at least 3-4 weeks in advance to secure the lowest rates. Enable the Fare Alert system on ixigo so we can monitor prices for you and notify you as soon as the fare drops. Traveling mid-week (Tuesdays and Wednesdays) is generally cheaper than weekend flights.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is flight ticket booking cheaper on ixigo?
ixigo compares flights across various sites to give you the lowest prices automatically. In addition, exclusive bank coupons, instant cashbacks, and flight booking offers allow you to save even more.
How can I find cheap flight tickets on ixigo?
